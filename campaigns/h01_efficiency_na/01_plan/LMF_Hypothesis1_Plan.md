# LMF Experiment Plan: Hypothesis 1 (Efficiency)

## 1. Hypothesis Definition
*   **Statement:** "Millennial professionals (25-40) will engage with **'Time Efficiency'** hooks because they value staying informed but lack time to read traditional news."
*   **Persona:** Millennial Professional (The "Morning Brew" Reader).
*   **Value Promise:** "Your Daily Market Briefing in 60 Seconds."
*   **Primary Metric (KPI):** CTR (Click-Through Rate) > 2.0%.

### 1.1 Validation Rationale (ICE Score)
*   **Score:** **9.0** (Highest Priority)
*   **Impact (9/10):** Target audience has high willingness to engage with time-saving utilities.
*   **Confidence (8/10):** Proven model by 'Morning Brew', 'Axios'.
*   **Ease (10/10):** Summary content is operationally easy to produce.

## 2. Target Audience (Meta Ads)
*   **Location:** North America (US, Canada)
*   **Age:** 25 - 40
*   **Gender:** All
*   **Interests:**
    *   Investment
    *   ETF (Exchange Traded Fund)
    *   The Wall Street Journal
    *   Morning Brew
    *   Passive income

## 3. Creative Strategy (Ad Type A)
*   **Concept:** "Market in a Minute"
*   **Competitor Benchmark:** Morning Brew (Witty/Brief), Robinhood Snacks (Snackable).
*   **Visual Direction:** 
    *   High-quality photo of a busy professional setting (e.g., coffee on desk, morning commute).
    *   Text Overlay: "Market Recap in 60s ⏱️"
    *   Vibe: Productive, Smart, Efficient.
*   **Copywriting:**
    *   **Headline:** Market recap in 60s. ⏱️
    *   **Primary Text:** Stop doomscrolling. Start earning. Get your daily market brief while you brew your coffee. No jargon, just signals.

## 4. Execution Steps
1.  **Asset Generation:** Create "Efficiency" themed image (Completed).
2.  **Campaign Setup:** Meta Ads Manager -> Traffic/Leads Objective.
3.  **Launch:** Set daily budget ($30/day) and run for 3 days.
4.  **Analysis:** Compare CTR against benchmark (2%).
